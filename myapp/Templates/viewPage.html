How It Works –
Simple & Efficient!

Easily access and manage timetables in just a few
steps. Whether you are a student checking your
schedule or an admin organizing classes, our
system makes it simple and efficient.

Admin Creates Timetables

Admins log in and enter course details, lecturers, and module slots.

Timetables Are Published Online

Once finalized, timetables are instantly available for students.

System Optimizes & Prevents Conflicts

The system auto-detects clashes and organizes a smooth schedule.

Students View & Download

Students search, view, and download their timetables anytime.





Simplifying Timetables: A Seamless Experience

The UCSY Timetable Maker makes it easy for both students and admins. Students can quickly view and download their semester schedules, while admins have the tools to create, modify, and duplicate class timetables with ease.
